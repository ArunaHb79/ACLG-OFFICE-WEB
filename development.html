About Development Department

The Development Department plays a crucial role in planning, implementing, and monitoring local government development projects across Hambantota district. We coordinate infrastructure development, community programs, and strategic planning initiatives that enhance the quality of life for local residents.

Our team works closely with local authorities, communities, and stakeholders to identify development needs and ensure effective implementation of projects that promote sustainable growth.

Our Services

Project Planning

Develop comprehensive development plans, identify project priorities, and coordinate resource allocation for local government initiatives.

Infrastructure Development

Oversee construction and improvement of roads, bridges, community facilities, and other essential infrastructure projects.

Progress Monitoring

Track project implementation, evaluate progress, and ensure timely completion of development initiatives within budget.

Community Programs

Coordinate community development programs, capacity building initiatives, and public participation in local development.

Strategic Planning

Formulate long-term development strategies aligned with national and provincial development goals and policies.

Impact Assessment

Evaluate the effectiveness and impact of completed projects to inform future development planning and decision-making.
