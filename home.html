About Our Office

The Assistant Commissioner of Local Government Office in Hambantota plays a crucial role in overseeing and coordinating local government activities within the district. Our office ensures effective implementation of government policies and programs at the local level.

We are dedicated to promoting good governance, transparency, and accountability while serving the needs of local authorities and communities in Hambantota.

Our Departments

Five specialized departments working together for excellence

Account Department

Manages financial operations, budget planning, and ensures proper allocation and utilization of resources for local government projects.

  • Financial Management
  • Budget Planning
  • Audit Coordination
  • Payment Processing

Establishment Department

Handles human resource management, staff appointments, promotions, and administrative matters related to personnel.

  • HR Management
  • Recruitment
  • Staff Development
  • Leave Administration

Development Department

Oversees development projects, infrastructure planning, and implementation of local government development initiatives.

  • Project Planning
  • Infrastructure Development
  • Progress Monitoring
  • Community Programs

Investigation Department

Conducts inquiries, investigations into administrative matters, and ensures compliance with regulations and procedures.

  • Administrative Inquiries
  • Compliance Monitoring
  • Report Preparation
  • Grievance Resolution

Engineering Department

Manages technical infrastructure projects, construction oversight, and engineering consultancy for local government facilities.
