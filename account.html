About Account Department

The Account Department is responsible for managing all financial operations within the Assistant Commissioner of Local Government Office, Hambantota. We ensure proper allocation, utilization, and monitoring of government funds for local development projects and administrative functions.

Our team maintains accurate financial records, processes payments, coordinates audits, and provides financial guidance to ensure transparency and accountability in all financial matters.

Our Services

Financial Management

Comprehensive management of all financial transactions, ensuring proper documentation and compliance with government financial regulations.

Budget Planning

Strategic budget formulation and allocation for various local government projects and administrative requirements.

Audit Coordination

Facilitate internal and external audits, ensuring all financial records are accurate and readily available for review.

Payment Processing

Efficient processing of payments to vendors, contractors, and staff, maintaining proper authorization protocols.

Expense Monitoring

Track and monitor all expenditures to ensure adherence to budget allocations and prevent overspending.

Financial Reporting

Prepare detailed financial statements and reports for management review and government submissions.
