About Establishment Department

The Establishment Department handles all human resource matters for the Assistant Commissioner of Local Government Office and associated local authorities in Hambantota district. We manage recruitment, appointments, promotions, transfers, and all personnel-related administrative functions.

Our team ensures compliance with public service regulations while fostering a productive and supportive work environment for all staff members.

Our Services

HR Management

Comprehensive human resource management including personnel records, performance evaluations, and staff welfare programs.

Recruitment

Manage recruitment processes, conducting interviews, and facilitating appointments in accordance with public service regulations.

Staff Development

Coordinate training programs, workshops, and professional development opportunities for staff enhancement.

Leave Administration

Process and manage all types of leave applications including annual, medical, and special leave requests.

Promotions & Transfers

Handle staff promotions, transfers, and administrative procedures ensuring fair and transparent processes.

Service Records

Maintain accurate and up-to-date personnel files, service records, and employment documentation.
