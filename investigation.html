About Investigation Department

The Investigation Department is responsible for conducting thorough administrative inquiries and ensuring compliance with government regulations and procedures across local authorities in Hambantota district. We investigate complaints, irregularities, and alleged misconduct to maintain transparency and accountability.

Our team works diligently to uphold good governance standards, resolve grievances, and recommend corrective actions when necessary, ensuring the integrity of local government operations.

Our Services

Administrative Inquiries

Conduct comprehensive investigations into administrative matters, complaints, and alleged irregularities in local government operations.

Compliance Monitoring

Ensure adherence to government regulations, policies, and procedures across all local authority functions and operations.

Report Preparation

Prepare detailed investigation reports with findings, evidence, and recommendations for appropriate action.

Grievance Resolution

Address public complaints and grievances through fair investigation processes and recommend suitable resolutions.

Misconduct Investigations

Investigate allegations of staff misconduct and recommend disciplinary actions in accordance with public service regulations.

Policy Recommendations

Provide recommendations for policy improvements based on investigation findings to prevent future irregularities.
