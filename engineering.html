About Engineering Department

The Engineering Department provides technical expertise and engineering services for local government infrastructure projects across Hambantota district. We are responsible for technical planning, design review, construction supervision, and maintenance of public facilities and infrastructure.

Our team of qualified engineers ensures that all construction projects meet technical standards, safety requirements, and quality specifications while providing professional engineering consultancy to local authorities.

Our Services

Technical Planning

Develop technical specifications, engineering designs, and feasibility studies for infrastructure and construction projects.

Construction Supervision

Provide on-site supervision and quality control for construction projects to ensure compliance with approved plans and standards.

Infrastructure Maintenance

Coordinate maintenance and repair of public infrastructure including buildings, roads, bridges, and drainage systems.

Engineering Consultancy

Provide expert technical advice and engineering consultancy services to local authorities on infrastructure projects.

Quality Assurance

Conduct inspections and testing to ensure construction quality, material standards, and structural integrity.

Contract Administration

Manage engineering contracts, evaluate contractor performance, and certify payments for completed work.
